Riddle Quest

Journey through Mysteries

Instructions

  • Embark on an Adventure:

    Prepare yourself for an exciting journey filled with intriguing riddles and challenges.

  • Explore Five Levels:

    Dive into five levels, each offering a unique set of riddles waiting to be solved. Discover new challenges and excitement at every turn.

  • Use Hints to Aid You:

    If you find yourself stuck, fear not! You have access to hints to help you along the way. However, you have a limited supply of 5 hints available for the entire game, so use them wisely.

  • Earn Points for Correct Answers:

    Solve each riddle to earn points. Each correct answer will reward you with 10 points.

  • Strive for Victory:

    Your objective is to conquer all levels and emerge victorious. Are you up for the challenge?

  • Let the Adventure Begin:

    Click "Start" to commence your Riddle Quest!
